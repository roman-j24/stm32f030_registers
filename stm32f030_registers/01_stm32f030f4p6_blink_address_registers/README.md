# pinout STM32F030F4P6
pin 15 - GND 
pin 16 - 3V3 
pin 19 - SWDIO 
pin 20 - SWCLK 

pin 2  - PF0 as out 
pin 6  - PA0 as out 
pin 14 - PB1 as out

#
The LED is connected through a resistor with a resistance of about 200 Ohms
